  • Set during the summer of 1985, Hawkins faces new threats as the Starcourt Mall opens, drawing attention away from the strange happenings in town. Eleven and Mike's relationship grows, while Dustin returns from camp with a new girlfriend, Suzie. Meanwhile, Russian scientists have secretly reopened the gate to the Upside Down beneath the mall, allowing the Mind Flayer to return and possess residents, including Billy Hargrove. The group must battle this new enemy, leading to a climactic showdown that results in Hopper's apparent death and Eleven losing her powers.
