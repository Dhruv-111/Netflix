  • In the small town of Hawkins, Indiana, a young boy named Will Byers goes missing. As his friends and family search for him, they encounter a mysterious girl with a shaved head and supernatural abilities. She goes by the name Eleven. As they delve deeper, they uncover a dark secret government lab, sinister creatures from an alternate dimension called the Upside Down, and the terrifying Demogorgon. The season ends with Will's rescue but hints at the lingering connection to the Upside Down.
